About the App

Many educators encounter challenges when manually recording daily student attendance across various sessions and classes.

In response to this common issue, we developed 'Attendify,' a web and mobile application built with AppSheet.

Attendify streamlines the process of managing student attendance, offering educators a simple and organized solution.

With Attendify, teachers can efficiently track daily student attendance, enhancing overall classroom management and administrative tasks.
